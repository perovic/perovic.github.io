About Me

Hi! My Name is Boris Perović, but you know that by now. I am a technological dreamer and computer lover. In one word, my expertise are systems, while my general interests range from theory of distributed computing to space technologies, AI and novel transportation methods. When focused on a problem, I might work very long hours alone, but I feel best around people and meeeting new ones is one of my passions. I continuously try to motivate those around me to achieve great things and I hope that after leaving this website you will be inspired to do the same.

Expertise and Experience

I have lived in three countries for now and attended three universities, each of which are amazing in their own right. Interning at NetLink Solutions gave me an insight into the fast-moving domain of online commerce and entrepreneurship, but also got me engaged in the open-source world and communities. Working at CERN got me in touch with the boundaries of human physics knowledge and scientific computing, and allowed me to work with some of the best people in the field. I love to challenge myself and work on hard problems that have a real-world impact.
