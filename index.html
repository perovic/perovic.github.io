Expertise and Experience

I have lived in three countries for now and attended three universities, each of which are amazing in their own right. Throughout my studies I worked on numerous academic projects and performed two internships. Interning at Net Link Solutions gave me an insight into the fast-moving domain of online commerce and entrepreneurship, but also got me engaged in the open-source world and communities around it. Working at CERN got me in touch with the boundaries of human physics knowledge and scientific computing, and allowed me to work with some of the best people in the field. I always try to remember the big picture, challenge myself and work on hard problems that have a real-world impact.
